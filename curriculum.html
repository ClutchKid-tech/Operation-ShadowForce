Training Curriculum

Our step-by-step spy modules for ages 9–16:

01. Code Name & Dossier

Learn how to choose your covert identity and record mission intel.

02. Language Lab

Basics of a secret language—French, Spanish, or custom codewords.

03. Morse Code 101

Master sending and decoding messages with taps and beeps.

04. Emergency Skills & First Aid

Essential drills: bandaging, splints, and creating an escape plan.

05. Disguise & Stealth

Simple props, makeup tricks, and moving undetected.

06. Navigation & Orienteering

Map reading, compass use, and covert route planning.

07. Encryption & Ciphers

Create and break simple ciphers to protect your intel.

08. Final Field Mission

Put your skills to the test in a team-based capstone operation.
